# excel-ai-risk-control-matrix
AI-powered Excel template for risk and control matrices in audit and compliance workflows

## Overview

The AI Risk and Control Matrix (AI RACM) is an open-source template designed for audit professionals, risk managers, compliance officers, and AI practitioners to assess and document risks associated with AI systems.

## Features

- Comprehensive risk categories for AI systems
- Control measures aligned with audit best practices
- Regulatory compliance mappings (EU AI Act, GDPR, SOX, etc.)
- Customizable for different industries and use cases
- Designed for professional audit documentation

## Getting Started

1. Download the Excel template from the releases section
2. Customize for your organization's needs
3. Document AI risks and implement controls
4. Use for audit evidence and compliance reporting

## Use Cases

- **Internal Audit**: AI system audits and risk assessments
- **Compliance**: Regulatory compliance documentation
- **Risk Management**: Enterprise AI risk frameworks
- **Governance**: AI governance and oversight programs
- **Consulting**: Client AI risk assessments
